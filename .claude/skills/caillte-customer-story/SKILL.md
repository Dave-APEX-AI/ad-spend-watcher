# caillte-customer-story — social proof + light system education

The trust pillar. Each post tells one customer's before→after and *lightly* teaches what
the system does — proof first, soft sell second. One of the 2 daily posts is a story.

## Story → post structure (carousel, ~6 slides — best for saves)
1. **Hook / the skeptic or situation** — name + trade + the doubt or problem.
   ("John runs a plumbing firm. He thought an AI receptionist was a gimmick.")
2. **The pain** — what it was costing them (missed calls / cold leads / dead website).
3. **What we did — lightly** — the system, in plain words, no jargon.
   ("So we started tiny: it only answered his after-hours calls.")
4. **The turn / result** — the moment it clicked + a concrete outcome.
5. **Where they are now** — the new normal.
6. **CTA** — relatable bridge to the reader + free audit.

## The three products (map every story to one)
- **AI receptionist** — catches the call (John).
- **Speed-to-lead automation** — instant reply/qualify on DMs/forms (Jamie).
- **Smart chat+voice website** — customers find + book 24/7 (Tom).

## Voice & rules
- Plain, specific, trade-native. Proof before pitch. Numbers where real; never invent them.
- Explain the system in ONE plain line — "an AI that answers every call and books the job",
  not "conversational AI platform".
- End on a bridge: "Sceptical too? Start with after-hours." — meet the reader's objection.

## ⚠️ Consent guardrail (important)
Real names/results need the customer's OK. Before posting a story with a real first name:
- Confirm the customer is happy to be named, OR
- Anonymise: "a plumber we work with in Cork", "an electrician on our books".
Never fabricate a result or a quote. If a number isn't confirmed, keep it qualitative.

## Formats
- Default: **carousel** (storytelling + saves). Also works as a **static** (one-card quote +
  result) or a **reel** (the story as 5 caption cards). Rotate formats across the week.

## Cadence
Stories are 1 of the 2 daily posts (the other = education/banter/maths/proof). See
`social/content/customer-stories.md` for the story bank + the 2/day month plan.
