# caillte-demo-clip — "watch it work" demo reels (£0)

The trust engine. Newcomer AI agencies *tell*; we *show* the receptionist booking a real
(or clearly-reconstructed) job. No tools to buy — you screen-record the product / a call.

## When to use
- Pillar-3 "Watch it work", Office Heroes "Receipts" / "Already Booked It" formats.
- Any time we need proof over claim.

## Output: a shot-list + script
Produce a tight plan:
1. **Hook (0–1.5s)** — sound-off pattern interrupt from `social/content/hooks.md`
   (e.g. "Turn the sound on — this isn't a person.") as a burned-in caption over the
   first frame. No slow intro, no logo sting.
2. **The recording** — what to capture:
   - Screen-rec of the dashboard/call log, OR a phone-call screen recording, OR a
     waveform of the AI's voice with live captions.
   - Show the *moment* it matters: rings → AI answers in 2 rings → books → deposit/time
     confirmed → owner did nothing.
3. **Caption overlays** — line-by-line, big, mobile-first, amber for the stakes ("9pm",
   "£420 job"), green for the win ("Booked.").
4. **End card** — green soundwave + "Don't let leads go *caillte*." + audit CTA.
5. **Post caption + hashtags** — hook first line, 1–2 lines of context, 1 soft CTA.

## Consent & honesty (non-negotiable)
- Use **real** calls only with consent; otherwise label as a **reconstruction**.
- Never fake a testimonial or invent a result. Redact customer details.

## Format specs
7–21s, 9:16 (1080×1920) for reels, burned-in captions, sound optional but rewardable,
strong first frame. Recut the same clip to 4:5 for feed. Mirror to Facebook.

## Tooling (all free)
Screen-record: macOS QuickTime / built-in; edit + captions: CapCut (free). For AI-actor
*reconstructions* at scale later, hand off to `caillte-ugc-video` (paid Arcads, Phase 2).
