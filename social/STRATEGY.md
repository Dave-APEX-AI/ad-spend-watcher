# CaillteAI — Instagram & Facebook Growth Strategy

**Action mode: GROW — and SHIP NOW.** Optimise every post for reach → saves/shares →
follows. Soft CTA only (free missed-call audit). Build the audience first, monetise second.

> ## ⚡ Operating principle: aggressive timelines, ship ASAP
> Default to the fastest path to *live*, not the tidiest plan. Done-today beats
> perfect-next-week. Concretely:
> - **Launch is measured in hours, not weeks.** First post goes up the same day the
>   assets exist. See `content/launch-sprint.md` — the 72-hour go-live plan.
> - **Front-load everything.** Batch a week of content in one sitting; never wait on a
>   "content day" that's three days out.
> - **Bias to publish.** An 8/10 post live today > a 10/10 post next week. Ship, watch,
>   iterate in public.
> - **No idle backlog.** If an asset is rendered, it gets scheduled the same day.
> - Every plan states a deadline in **hours/days**, and the next concrete action is always
>   something doable *right now*.

## The thesis

Trades owners don't search for "AI receptionist" — they *feel* the pain of missed
calls and lost jobs. So we don't sell software; we **dramatise the pain and quantify
it**, then show a calm, specific fix. Faceless, daily, trade-native. The flood of
generic "AI agencies" can't do trade-native pain at volume. We can.

## Platform roles

- **Instagram (primary):** Reels for reach (cold audience), carousels for saves/authority,
  Stories for warm nurture + DMs. This is the growth engine.
- **Facebook (primary):** Same content, reposted natively. FB still over-indexes on the
  exact demo (older trades owners, group culture). Reels cross-post; also drop value
  posts into relevant trades Groups (no spam — answer questions, link sparingly).
- **LinkedIn:** explicitly out of scope. Ignore.

## Cadence (sustainable, faceless, £0)

- **Reels: 1/day** (the growth lever — IG rewards reel frequency for new accounts).
- **Carousels: 3–4/week** (the save/authority lever).
- **Stories: 3–5 frames/day** (polls, "missed-call counter", behind-the-AI, reposts).
- **FB: mirror everything** + 2 Group value-drops/week.

If a day must be skipped, skip a carousel, never the reel.

## The hook is 90% of it

Sound-off, thumb-stopping first 1.5s. Every piece opens with a **pattern interrupt + a
number or a pain**. Hooks live in `content/hooks.md`; the `caillte-hook-writer` skill
generates and grades them. Rule: if the hook doesn't make a stressed plumber stop
scrolling, the post doesn't ship.

## Algorithm tactics (2026, faceless-safe)

- **Reels 7–21s**, 4:5 or 9:16, burned-in captions, trending-but-subtle audio, strong
  first frame (no slow intros, no logo stings).
- **Carousels:** slide 1 is a hook you'd screenshot; last slide = soft CTA + "save this".
  Carousels get re-served to non-followers when saved — our authority engine.
- **Hook in the caption too** (first line before "more"), then value, then 1 CTA.
- **Keywords, not just hashtags:** IG SEO now reads on-screen text + captions. Say
  "missed calls", "plumber", "electrician", "trades business" in words.
- **Hashtags:** 3–5 specific > 30 generic. Mix: niche (#plumbinguk #sparky #tradesni),
  geo (#ukandireland), pain (#missedcalls). Rotate; avoid banned/giant tags.
- **Reply to every comment in the first 60 min** (engagement window). Pin a comment with
  the CTA. Use the comment section to seed the missed-call audit.
- **Repurpose ruthlessly:** one carousel → a reel (slides as frames) → 3 story polls →
  an FB post. One idea, five posts.
- **Collabs/duets:** stitch trade-fail clips, react to Tradify/Joe-Doyle-style content
  (respectfully), tag suppliers and tools — borrow audiences.

## Funnel (GROW mode)

```
Reach (reels)  →  Authority/Save (carousels)  →  Warm (stories)  →
Soft CTA: free missed-call audit  →  DM keyword "CAILLTE"  →  list + demo
```

The audit (`content/lead-magnet-missed-call-audit.md`) is the *only* offer we push,
and gently. It captures intent without breaking GROW mode.

## Targets (aggressive — front-loaded, not vanity-locked)

| Metric | **72 hrs** | Day 7 | Day 30 | Day 90 |
|--------|-----------|-------|--------|--------|
| Live & posting | profile live + 3 posts up | **2 posts/day** held | held | held |
| Followers | first 50 | +150–400 | +1–2.5k | +6–10k |
| Median reel views | 300–1k | 1–3k | 3–8k | 10–25k+ |
| Saves/reel (carousels) | 5+ | 20+ | 50+ | 100+ |
| Audit completions | first 1–3 | 10+ | 40+ | 150+ |
| Booked demos from social | — | 1–2 | 5–10 | 20–30 |

Pace is deliberately hot: **2 posts/day** from Day 7 (1 reel + 1 carousel/static), not 1.
Leading indicators we steer on daily: **hold rate (first 3s), saves, shares, profile
visits → follows** via `caillte-daily-snapshot`.

## Operating rhythm (compressed)

- **Today:** run `content/launch-sprint.md` — profile live + first 3 posts up. No waiting.
- **Daily (15 min):** `caillte-daily-snapshot` → post the day's 1–2 pieces, reply to every
  comment in the first 60 min, log numbers.
- **One batch night/week (45 min):** render + schedule the whole next week in Meta Business
  Suite. Never produce day-of unless reacting to a trend.
- **Mid-week pulse:** kill losing formats immediately, recut the week's winner same day —
  don't wait for a Friday review.

## Guardrails

- Never post a generic motivational/guru clip with no trade specificity.
- Never fake a testimonial or a call recording — use real (consented) or clearly-labelled
  reconstructions.
- Stay in trade idiom; if a post could be from any "AI agency", kill it.
- One offer (the audit). Don't dilute GROW mode with multiple CTAs.
