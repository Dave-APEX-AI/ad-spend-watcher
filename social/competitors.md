# Competitive & Inspiration Map

## Aspirational creators (model the *mechanic*, not the message)

### Joe Doyle (@joedoyleentrepreneur) — ~34k IG / 85k+ across platforms
- Ireland's #1 business mentor for tradesmen; ex-Dragons' Den; raw talking-head reels,
  podcast, book. Built a loyal trades audience by speaking *only* to trade owners' money
  problems, relentlessly and plainly.
- **Steal:** the niche-obsession (he only talks to tradesmen), the recurring franchise
  (*Office Heroes*), the raw/high-frequency cadence, the "I get your world" tone.
- **Don't steal:** his face/personal-brand model (we're faceless), his exact scripts,
  his mentor positioning. We sell a product, not mentorship.
- **Relationship:** *not a competitor* — adjacent. Long game: his audience IS our
  audience. React to/duet his themes respectfully; he could be a partner/feature later.

## Adjacent platforms (audiences to tap, not fight)

### Tradify (job management software for trades)
- Big, beloved tool in the same niche (quoting, scheduling, invoicing). Strong social.
- **Play:** we're *complementary*, not competitive — Tradify runs the job; CaillteAI
  catches the call that creates it. Content angle: "Tradify manages the job. But who's
  answering the phone while you're on it?" Tag/duet their content; pursue integration
  story ("answered call → booked straight into your job software").
- Others in this orbit to tap similarly: **ServiceM8, Jobber, Powered Now, Commusoft,
  simPRO, BigChange, Fergus** — plus trade suppliers, toolbox/banter pages, federations.

## Direct competitors (the flood)

### "AI receptionist / AI agency" newcomers (popping up daily)
- Generic AI-agency accounts selling "AI receptionists/chatbots/automation" to *anyone*.
  Mostly: stock visuals, hype captions ("🚀 revolutionise your business with AI"), no
  vertical, no proof, founder-face churn.
- **Their weaknesses → our wedge:**
  | Them | Us |
  |------|-----|
  | Vertical-agnostic ("any business") | Trades-only, trade idiom |
  | Hype, no numbers | The missed-call maths, real demos |
  | Founder-face, low cadence | Faceless franchise, daily |
  | "AI agency" | "The trades' AI receptionist" |
  | Tell | **Show it answer a real call** |
- **How we win:** out-specific and out-publish them. Trade-native pain at daily volume is
  the moat. The minute a post could've come from any AI agency, we've lost — kill it.

## Positioning statement (north star)

> For UK & Irish trades owners who lose jobs to missed calls, **CaillteAI** is the AI
> voice receptionist *built for the trades* — it answers every call, books the job, and
> never has a bad day. Unlike generic AI agencies, we speak your trade, show the receipts,
> and put a real number on what missed calls are costing you.
