## Repurposing rule (one idea → five posts)

Every carousel → recut as a reel (slides as frames) → 3 story polls → 1 FB post →
1 pinned-comment hook. You only need ~6 *original* ideas a week.

cut the rest, and let the winners set Week 5+. Hold the 1-reel/day line — consistency is
the whole game.
