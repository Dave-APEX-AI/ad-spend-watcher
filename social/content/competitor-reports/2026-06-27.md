## Format & hook trends (late June 2026)

- **Mix:** 60–70% reels / 20–30% carousels / <10% static is the 2026 consensus. Reels win reach (~36% more); carousels win engagement/saves (https://contentdrips.com/blog/2026/06/instagram-reels-vs-carousels-2026-guide/, https://sproutsocial.com/insights/instagram-trends/). For a GROW account, stay reel-first.
- **DM shares now weighted 3–5× higher than likes** for reel distribution — make reels people *send to a mate* (e.g. tag-your-plumber-mate bait) (https://buffer.com/resources/instagram-algorithms/).
- **Length:** 15–30s for highest completion; tension/"wait for it" builds reward 40–48s. Keep utility reels tight.
- **Hooks:** text-on-first-frame is non-negotiable — *"Wait for it…"*, *"This changed everything…"*, and Joe-Doyle-style *"Every [tradesman]…"* universal-truth openers (https://later.com/blog/instagram-reels-trends/).
- **Audio:** ride rising sounds *before* they peak. This week: "Apple" (Charli XCX) ~200k videos; **"Game Time" (Future & Tyla) — FIFA World Cup 2026 soundtrack, all over sports/anticipation edits** (https://buffer.com/resources/trending-audio-instagram/). World Cup is the timely culture hook this week.
- **Originality penalty:** IG is de-ranking reposted/watermarked/recycled content — original faceless reels are favoured.
