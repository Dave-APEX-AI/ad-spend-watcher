## What to look for each week
- **Formats:** reels vs carousels vs statics vs talking-head; lengths.
- **Hooks:** opening lines/styles that recur or visibly pop.
- **Cadence:** how often they post; which days/times.
- **Performing:** posts with visibly high likes/comments/views (public only).
- **Gaps:** pain/angles nobody covers that suit our missed-call wedge.
