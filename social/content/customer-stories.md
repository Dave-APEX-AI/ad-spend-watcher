# Customer success stories — the social-proof pillar

The highest-trust content in our niche (the competitor scan confirmed nobody does proof
well). One of the **2 daily posts** is a story. Each shows the result AND lightly explains
the system. Built with `caillte-customer-story`.

## ⚠️ Consent first
Before any story posts with a real first name, confirm the customer is happy to be named —
or anonymise ("a plumber we work with"). Never invent results or quotes. The three below
use first-name + trade only (no company/location); confirm or anonymise before they ship.

## The 2-a-day cadence
- **09:00 UTC — story slot** (proof). **18:00 UTC — other type** (maths/banter/quick-win/
  demo/Office Hero/reel). Two different types every day.
- Queue is interleaved so each day pairs a story with a non-story (see `queue/schedule.json`).

## Making 3 stories cover a month (honestly)
We have **3 real stories** — we won't fabricate customers. To sustain the daily story slot
without making things up, rotate each real story across **formats and angles**:
- 3 stories × 3 formats (carousel / static quote / reel) = **9 story posts**.
- Plus angle variants (the sceptic angle, the result angle, the "how it works" angle).
- Fill remaining story-slots with **proof-style content** that isn't a named customer:
  anonymised mini-stories, real call recordings (with consent), the missed-call maths,
  before/after demos.
- **The real unlock:** feed me 1–2 new customer stories a week and the bank compounds.
  Easiest source — a quick voice note from Dave after a good result.
