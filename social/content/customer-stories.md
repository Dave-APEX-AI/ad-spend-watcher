# Customer success stories — the social-proof pillar (3/week)

The highest-trust content in our niche. One of the **2 daily posts** is a story; we add
**3 new stories per week**, woven among the other post types.

## Compliance & honesty (the line we hold)
- **Anonymise by default:** "a plumber we work with", "a solar installer we work with".
  No real names/company/location unless the customer has explicitly consented.
- **No fabricated specifics:** never invent a £ figure, %, or a direct quote and present it
  as a real customer's result. Keep outcomes **qualitative** ("booked jobs he'd never have
  seen", "leads booked while still warm"). This keeps us inside UK rules on testimonials
  (DMCCA 2024 / ASA) — fake reviews/testimonials are illegal.
- **Real numbers strengthen it:** when Dave confirms an actual result/quote for a customer,
  we can add it (still anonymised) — that's when a story goes from good to undeniable.
- We describe **what the system genuinely does for that trade** (true & demonstrable),
  dressed as a story — not invented metrics.

## The 3/week engine (recurring process)
Each week, pick **3 trades** not used recently and produce one anonymised story each
(`caillte-customer-story` → carousel), then weave into the queue (story in the PM slot).
- Rotate trades so the feed stays varied and every trade sees itself represented.
- Rotate the angle (sceptic / overwhelmed / dead-website / storm-surge / grant-flood).
- Reuse a strong story later in a **different format** (static quote, reel) — fresh, not
  repetitive.
- Like the competitor watch, this is **triggered, not autonomous**: say "add this week's
  stories" (or I batch a few weeks ahead). I can pre-build 2–3 weeks at a time so the
  queue stays full hands-off.
