# Hook Swipe File

Sound-off, first 1.5s, pattern-interrupt + a number or a pain. Use as reel openers,
carousel slide-1s, and caption first-lines. The `caillte-hook-writer` skill generates
and grades new ones against these patterns. Rule: if it doesn't stop a stressed plumber
mid-scroll, bin it.

## Missed-call pain (pillar 1)

- "Your phone rang 11 times today. You answered 4. That's 7 jobs you'll never know you lost."
- "9:42pm. Burst pipe. She's panicking. You're asleep. So she rings the next plumber."
- "The most expensive sound in your business: a phone ringing out."
- "You didn't lose that job on price. You lost it because you didn't pick up."
- "Every missed call is a customer ringing your competitor next."
- "£280 average job × 9 missed calls a week × 50 weeks = go on, do the maths."
- "You're on a roof. The phone's in the van. There goes a £600 job."
- "Voicemail is where leads go to die."
- "The job didn't go to the best plumber. It went to the first one who answered."

## The maths (pillar 2)

- "What one missed call a day actually costs you over a year. (Sit down.)"
- "I added up a tradesman's missed calls for a week. He nearly cried."
- "Missed calls aren't a £0 problem. Here's the real number 👇"
- "Your busiest competitor isn't better. They just answer the phone."

## Watch it work (pillar 3)

- "I let an AI answer a plumber's phone for a week. Here's what happened."
- "This is an AI booking a job at 11pm while the owner's asleep. Watch."
- "Real call. AI receptionist. Booked, deposit taken, owner did nothing."
- "Turn the sound on. This isn't a person."

## Trade-native banter (pillar 4)

- "Things that ring while you're elbow-deep in a sink: everything."
- "POV: you climb down the ladder and there's 3 missed calls and a voicemail."
- "Tradesmen will buy a £400 impact driver but answer the phone like it owes them money."
- "The missed call's POV: 'great week, funded the plumber down the road's holiday.'"
- "Your missus doing the books shouldn't also be your receptionist."

## Quick win / how-to (pillar 5)

- "5 calls you're losing every week without realising (and how to catch them)."
- "Do this one thing before 9pm and stop losing after-hours jobs."
- "The 3 words every trade business should say when the phone goes (it's not 'hello')."
- "Steal this missed-call follow-up text. Wins back ~1 in 4."

## Brand / name (pillar 6 / Office Heroes)

- "In Irish, 'caillte' means lost. Every missed call is a lost job. We're named after the
  thing we kill."
- "Meet the only employee who answers every call, never sleeps, and never has a bad day."
- "'Already booked it.' — the AI, while you were up a ladder."

## Hook formulas (for generating more)

1. **Number + loss:** "[real number] is what [specific missed call] just cost you."
2. **Time-stamp + scene:** "[9:42pm]. [Emergency]. You're [unavailable]. So they [ring a rival]."
3. **Contrarian:** "You didn't lose it on [price]. You lost it on [not answering]."
4. **POV/relatable:** "POV: you [trade scene] and [phone disaster]."
5. **Demo dare:** "Turn the sound on — this isn't a person."
6. **Maths tease:** "What [tiny thing] actually costs you over a year. (Sit down.)"
