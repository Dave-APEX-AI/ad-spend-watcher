# Pattern-Breakers — 10 algo-safe ideas that break the niche's saturated patterns

Output of "Prompt 1" (senior IG growth strategist). Niche: AI voice receptionists +
automation for UK & Irish trades. Audience: owner-operators on the tools. Goal: GROW
(reach/saves/follows → free missed-call audit).

## The 7 saturated patterns we deliberately avoid
1. Guru talking-head "why your business is failing" lectures (Joe-Doyle clones)
2. Generic AI hype + stock robot b-roll + faceless VO
3. Revenue/income flex screenshots
4. Generic tips listicles ("5 tools every tradesman needs")
5. Obviously-staged fake call demos
6. Hustle-porn motivational quotes over jobsite drone shots
7. Engagement bait ("tag a mate", point-at-floating-text dance)

## 10 pattern-breakers
| # | Idea | Hook | Format | Why fresh | Saves/shares |
|---|------|------|--------|-----------|--------------|
| 1 | The Missed-Call Receipt | "This missed call cost a plumber £680. Here's the receipt." | Image/2-slide | Real evidence, not staged | Screenshot wake-up call |
| 2 | Villain POV: "I'm your missed call" | "11 missed calls this week. I funded the plumber down the road's holiday." | Faceless reel | Antagonist POV, unused in niche | Dark-funny + relatable |
| 3 | The Silent Demo | "No voiceover. Just an AI booking a £420 job at 11pm. Sound on." | Screen-rec reel | Anti-hype proof | "That's not a person?" comments |
| 4 | "Caillte" word-of-the-day | "In Irish, caillte means lost. Every missed call is a lost job." | Bold static/3-slide | Cultural hook, not a tip | Irish-pride share, memorable |
| 5 | Reverse listicle | "5 calls you lost today without realising." | Carousel | Loved format, inverted to pain | Self-diagnostic = saves |
| 6 | "Don't hire us" anti-pitch | "3 trades who should NOT get an AI receptionist." | Carousel/reel | Reverse psychology | "That's me / not me" comments |
| 7 | The £ Sound | "The most expensive sound in your business 🔊" | Short reel, sound-on | Sound-design, not talking head | Sensory + clever = shares |
| 8 | Split-screen 9pm | "9pm. One of you is working. It's not you." | Split reel | Product as relief, not robot | Aspirational calm = saves |
| 9 | Office Hero of the Week | "Office Hero of the Week: answered 47 calls while Steve was on a roof." | Series static/reel | A ritual/franchise, not a flex | Recurring = follows |
| 10 | Honest cost breakdown | "I added up a tradesman's missed calls for a week. He nearly cried." | Carousel → audit | Vulnerability + real numbers | Useful maths = #1 save trigger |
